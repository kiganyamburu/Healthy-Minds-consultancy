Healthy Minds

Human behavior flows from three main sources: desire, emotion, and knowledge. Plato

Unknowingly, we humans experience each of the behavioral patterns in our day-to-day lives, some behaviors bring out rays of hope, while the others stroke displeasing darkness. Adhering to the different behavioral patterns we come across in our daily lives, seldom, we sulk in ourselves over actions that are most displeasing, emotionally unbearable, and psychologically unendurable to us.

Therefore, we have created a place that can be a home to you, our couches your comfort cushions, and the team your most adored friend, and most importantly ‘Healthy Minds’ your composed space.

We at ‘Healthy Minds’ assure you that we are your safe and tranquil place for you to share your stories and emotions without being judged or criticized. We lend our shoulders to lean on whenever you need us.

About us

Healthy Minds

Healthy Minds Counseling Centre is a mental health care /counseling center dedicated to help those who are struggling silently with their mental well-being. We help and support people who are emotionally unstable and/or dealing with depression, anxiety oroverwhelming stress.

What We Do:
Practice Cognitive Behavioral Therapy ( CBT)
Work closely with Adults and Teenagers
Provide workshops on mental health and stress management
